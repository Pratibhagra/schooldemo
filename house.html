    HOUSE AGNI

    "SUCCESS BEGINS WITH A PERSON'S WILL IT'S ALL IN THE STATE OF MIND".

    Agni House is in Yellow dress, a brightness that is often described as cheerful and warm. The inter house competitions, which were conducted, fostered a healthy spirit of competitions as well as strong sense of unity among the members of the house. In the various events the performance of the house was up to the expected standard and we gave a tough fight to other houses. The year 2015-16 has taught us to accept the moments of success and those of failure with indomitable spirit. Agni House has done exceedingly well in almost all events.

    So far the house students have brought laurels to this house in the various competitions held in this year

    The team spirit, confidence and co-operation of the students as well as the house tutors are set to make history at SGI. We hope in the coming years victory will embrace us more than this We thank our Principal Madam for the faith that she has kept in me. Once again a big thank you from the bottom of my heart to all the tutors and students.

    HOUSE TRISHUL

    “A TEAM IS NOT A GROUP OF PEOPLE WHO WORK TOGETHER. IT IS A GROUP OF PEOPLE WHO TRUST ONE ANOTHER.” Hi everyone…! It is a great pride and privilege for me to write this report on TRISHUL HOUSE as a House Master. All the Trishul House students participated enthusiastically and came out with flying colours through their wonderful results in all the Interhouse competitions conducted in the school. With a great discipline, the students contributed immensely not only in the cultural events, but also in sports to bring laurels to their own House. I take much pleasure to mention some names of our House students, who participated in CBSE Cluster, State and National level sports events.     HOUSE PRITHVI

    PRITHVI HOUSE’s greatest glory is not in falling but in rising every time we fall. Prithvi house is green color which symbolizes “Prosperity and Harmony”. This year came with lot of ideas, challenges and strenuous work. It’s not been easy for prithvians to achieve glory with strong competitors without hard work and support of all the tutors and house master. We only believed in fare game, building team spirit inculcating good values on the field and off the field. The encouragement that the house received made us to put our best efforts on the field. Our football boys’ team made a marvelous opening by being winner in both junior and senior category. Girls team added to the glory by being the runners up. In the aquatholan meet, Disha Parle, Vaishnavi Andhare, Umid Gaikwad, Laxman and Sabil made us proud. In Skating, the skaters Saisharan Patil, Nikita Pabari, Disha Parle, shahid Sheik, Runal Bhosale, Shridhar Mali,Aryan Kalloli, Mehak Bothra gave their best performance. Badminton was a setback for our house but that did not curb our desire to be winners. We moved ahead with positive vibes and became the winners of basketball tournament both in girls and senior boys. Swimming added another feather to the cap .In hockey, the boys team made it by being the winners. In table tennis and tennis we had to be satisfied with runners up position. Our junior cricket team breathes hard and emerged as winner while seniors emerged as runners up. Kabbadi, an indigenous game which was introduced this year made a final impact on the house points by being a winner. Pruthvians achieved tremendous success in the field of sports and also curved a niche in co-cuuricular activities. In coloring and poster making competition our students exhibited their love for color, bagged prizes and received appreciation. They displayed their talents in dance, solo singing, and declamation and brought laurels to the house by being the winners in dance and singing. The unique talent that our students have, helped us to gain popularity among all. Our students keep themselves updated with the latest information and this was proved through quiz competition. Technology has become a part of one’s life. Our students participated in PPT presentation and bagged second position. Each student participated actively in all activities, thus exhibiting teamwork, leadership, strong determination and above all trust and faith in each other. These qualities paved way to success and will help us to climb the ladder of success for many years to come. I wish good luck to all the students and extend my gratitude to all the house tutors for kind support directly and indirectly.

    HOUSE AKASH

    "The strength of the team is each individual member. The strength of each member is the team."

    Akash house emerged as a phoenix bird as compared to last year. the be inning of the academic year 2013-2014, Akash house has maintained a marked edge in all the interhouse competitions conducted.

    Students of Akash house excelled especially in co-curricular activities and also they proved that they are not less than anyone in track and field too. Challenges are what make life interesting; overcoming them is what makes life meaningful. In the race to win the Champions trophy we are ahead of others and we will put all our efforts together in the coming days to realize our goal.

    "In order to succeed, your desire for success should be greater than your fear of failure." Akash house participated in all the interhouse competitions with this motto and there was a positive vibration from the beginning of the year and the outcome was really visible in the results with the students of Akash house winning maximum number of prizes in each event conducted.

    There is a long list of Prize Winners of Akash house. I will not be able to mention all. From the abundance of my heart I congratulate all those who won and also participated in different competitions with passion and spirit. "If you have an apple and I have an apple and we exchange apples then you and I will still each have one apple.
