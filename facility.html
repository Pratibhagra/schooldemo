    CATERING FACILITY

    Parents should rest assured when they send their children to TPS because every student is cared for at all times. In school, all teachers & tutors in addition to carefully selected and trained experienced teachers who are entrusted with the responsibility of caring for the students, there are heads of department, curriculum coordinators, boarding house masters and house parents. There is availability of a resident doctor to take care of the health need of the Boarders 24 hours.

    At TPS students have access to quality, all-round education and personal attention from teachers who are recruited from all over the country

    SPORTS FACILITY

    At TPS various sporting activities like Football, Cricket, Skating, Volleyball, Swimming, Tennis, Badminton etc. are provided.

    Our physical education staffs are highly committed to fitness and wellness. They have vast experience in drawing out the best sporting instincts in students. Team work and sportsmanship skills are at the heart of the programme.
